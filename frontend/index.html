Architecture Overview

  1. Raw datasets enter from data/raw and are validated.
  2. DataCleaner and FeatureEngineer transform and enrich records.
  3. FlightPricePipeline applies numeric and categorical preprocessing.
  4. Modeling stage trains candidate regressors and stores outputs.
  5. Insights layer reports KPI metrics, trends, and operational logs.

Technology Stack
